Description

The Kayfun V5 Kayfun 5 style RTA is an updated edition of Kayfun Mini V3 style RTA.

It comes with more airflow, more juice control, and a redesigned deck. The Kayfun V5 style RTA features 4ml e-liquid capacity with glass tank, and 5ml capacity with SS sleeve.

This great quality Tobeco Clone is a well made 1:1 edition of the expensive high end German designed original.

It is an easy to use, top filling rebuildable tank atomiser, with an adjustable, four-position, airflow system and controllable, pressure-based, juice flow feeding system. You are able to access the re-designed build deck without emptying the juice reservoir and the optimised evaporation chamber gives intense flavour.

Wash the unit before use. This is to rid of machine oil or any other left over from production process. Washing the Tank / Atomizer is something that should be done for any new device
